A healthy amount of curiosity motivated my enrollment into the Data Analytics Boot Camp program at UC Berkley Extension. With so much data available in the world today; gaining some knowledge on what can be done by the use of different tools, added just enough motivation to pause from an actively engaging career to learn and appreciate the acquisition of new skills, in the pursue of exciting new projects opportunities.

Apart from the continued growth in technical skills, the program also offers opportunities to meet with other like-minded people and field experienced instructors. As well as provide a collaborative environment to contribute towards the common goals set out by the program. With an accelerated pace, a broad range of topics are discussed, sometimes only at a surface level for a general understanding of current trends and technologies. The rich curriculum offers a variety of subjects at an introductory level.
